About Me

I have always loved and thrived in creative spaces. When the capability to create something is in front of me, it is hard for my imagination not to run wild. I grew up playing video games, captivated by the landscapes, stories, detailing, and creativity that went into building those worlds.

I am a hands-on learner who likes to dive into all forms of creative expression, from poetry and music to drawing and 3D modeling. I place a lot of my passion into physical expression as well through running track and field and working out. I like to keep myself busy whether that means volunteering for an event committee, serving as a counselor for residents, or sitting on the board of a club organization.

I do not set limits, I set goals, and even if not every goal is reached, if at least one is obtained that is an achievement in itself.

What Drives Me

My love for video game worlds, immersive storytelling, and imaginative design has naturally drawn me toward animation and entertainment. I am fascinated by the way strong ideas can become living experiences, and I want to keep building work that feels thoughtful, expressive, and deeply human.

RA Health Awareness Campaign

As a Residential Advisor at The College of Wooster, I organized and executed a creative health awareness event that combined education with interactive fun. Working alongside fellow RAs and the campus Health and Wellness Center, I hosted a Mario Kart event where participants wore drunk simulation goggles while playing, creating an engaging and memorable way to educate students on the dangers and legal consequences of driving under the influence. The event attracted over 100 attendees and delivered a 30% increase in audience engagement.

Key Highlights

  • Partnered with the Health and Wellness Center for educational programming
  • Attracted 100+ attendees

  • Demonstrated ability to make serious topics accessible and engaging

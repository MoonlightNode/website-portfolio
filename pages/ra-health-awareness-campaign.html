RA Health Awareness Campaign

This project centered on communicating important health information in a way that felt approachable, respectful, and easy to understand. The goal was to turn a serious subject into something that could reach and resonate with a wider audience.

Approach

I focused on audience-centered messaging, clean visual layouts, and digital content that supported education rather than overload. Each asset was designed to make the message feel clear, truthful, and human.

Clear messaging and thoughtful design can make sensitive subjects feel more accessible and human.

Outcome

The campaign strengthened awareness efforts by pairing thoughtful design with strong storytelling. It demonstrated how communication can be both informative and emotionally engaging.

Project Snapshot

  • Role: Content strategist and campaign designer
  • Tools: Canva, Adobe Creative Suite
  • Focus: Health education and awareness

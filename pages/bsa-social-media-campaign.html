BSA Social Media Campaign

This campaign focused on building a stronger online presence through intentional storytelling, brand-friendly visuals, and consistent posting that connected with students and community audiences.

What I did

I created content concepts, promotional graphics, short-form video assets, and campaign messaging that aligned with the organization’s tone and goals. The work emphasized clear communication, accessible design, and a steady rhythm of engagement.

Creative storytelling paired with intentional design helped turn everyday updates into meaningful engagement.

Impact

The campaign helped increase event visibility and audience engagement by creating more compelling digital touchpoints. It also showed how thoughtful content can strengthen both brand identity and community connection.

Project Snapshot

  • Role: Content creator and digital marketer
  • Tools: Canva, Adobe Creative Suite, CapCut
  • Focus: Social media growth and audience engagement
